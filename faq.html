Get Started

1. What is the product range khakrawala offer?
We offer all type Snacks specialised in Different Khakra's, Namkeens, chivda, Dietpuffs, Chikki, Health bars etc. You can always refer to our products column in our website.
2. Does the taste and quality of products remains consistent?
we always take proper care of selecting the raw materials with good quality. As we understand the value of customers we take utmost care about quality and taste of products.
1. How much should be the size of the Shop?
Shop size can range from 30 Sq. Ft. to 150 Sq. Ft.
2. How much will be the Minimum investment to start the store?
The minimum investment will be ranging from 1-2 lacs. Which includes Furniture cost, signage, and food material. All applicable taxes extra.
3. What is the electricity requirement to run the store?
Single Phase electricity connection will be enough.
4. How many workers/ Helpers required?
It depends upon your individual. But With minimum of 2 manpower you can run the shop.
5. What should be the ideal location for setting up the store?
It should be a crowded place ideally near Vegetable Market, station roads, food market, near schools etc.
6. Can we keep products of other brands?
No Khakrawal comprise of more than 180 products, if required franchisee should consult with the company. Any other products found in store will lead to termination of franchise agreement.
7. Franchisee Agreement will be for how many years?
Franchisee Agreement will be for 3 years.
8. Store place should be own shop or rented?
If rented then rental agreement should be of 3 yrs. If Owned shop then it will be an added advantage.
9. Why one should take franchisee of Khakrawala?
Lower Franchisee fees Better quality of products and taste superior than others Low cost of raw material thus increasing profit Impressive, Aesthetical & convenient design of store. Gross margins of approx. 35%- 40%(higher than any other business brand in the category).
1. How much quantity one should look for distribution?
You can take as much as one can sell. As we have good enough production capacity.
2. What are the products available for distribution?
We offer range of all products for distribution.
3. Is there any criteria for taking distributorship?
Please contact us for more details.
